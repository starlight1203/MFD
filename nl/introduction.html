Inleiding

Our Background
It happened that we were walking out in the countryside of the US, reveling in the fascinating scenery and the scent of freshly cut grass in the air, when suddenly we heard a crisp tinkling sound. We looked around and realized it was coming from a wind chime hanging from the eaves of a home. We stopped and listened to the melodious notes. Perfectly mingled with nature, the delicate sound was wondrous, light, and emotionally evocative. That was the beginning of our story.

Our Vision
Dionysus Artcrafts Co., LTD. was founded by a group of like-minded friends who want to help people hold on to the particular moments in their lives. We believe that a warm sentence or a touching scene that flashes by is something that people wish to keep in their hearts forever. For this reason, we have come up with a variety of gifts that help people remember those precious moments, whether it is the sweet notes that touch the heart, the brilliant colors in the sunlight, or a heart-warming sentence that encourages you when you are in a bad mood. The perfect gift at the right moment can bind a memory in the heart for eternity.

What kind of gift does that special someone like best? What type of design can create the most resonance? Design and production are our strengths, and through them, we help our customers communicate with consumers. With a design team that boasts 20 years of experience and our factory in Jinhua China, we professionally design, make samples, and produce a wide range of gift products. Our products are shipped to the US, Germany, Belgium, and Italy to offer your customers the perfect gift to keep those precious moments in their hearts. We can make products that comply with your designs if you wish for exclusive, custom-made items. It's time to keep those treasured moments. Contact us now.
